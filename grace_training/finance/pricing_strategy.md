# GRACE Pricing Strategy 2025

## Executive Summary

GRACE's pricing strategy is designed to capture value across market segments while maintaining competitive positioning and supporting our growth objectives. Our tiered pricing model balances accessibility for mid-market customers with premium offerings for enterprise clients.

## Pricing Philosophy

### Core Principles
1. **Value-based pricing**: Price reflects customer ROI, not just cost
2. **Transparent and predictable**: No hidden fees, clear upgrade paths
3. **Scalable**: Pricing grows with customer usage and value
4. **Competitive**: Positioned as premium but justified by superior capabilities
5. **Flexible**: Customizable for enterprise needs

## Pricing Tiers

### Starter Tier
**Target Segment**: Small businesses, startups, proof-of-concept  
**Price**: $5,000/month ($50,000/year if paid annually, save 17%)

**Included**:
- Up to 10 active agents
- 100GB data processing/month
- Standard self-healing (response time: 15 min)
- Basic governance dashboard
- Email support (24-hour response)
- 1 user seat included, $200/month per additional seat
- API access (10,000 calls/month)
- Community forum access

**Limitations**:
- No custom integrations
- Standard SLA: 99.5% uptime
- 30-day audit log retention
- Single environment (production only)

**Ideal For**:
- Companies with 50-200 employees
- Testing GRACE capabilities
- Basic automation needs
- Limited compliance requirements

**Customer Examples**: E-commerce startups, small SaaS companies

## FAQs

**Q: Why not offer a free tier?**  
A: Free tier would attract low-value customers. Our Starter tier ($5k/month) is accessible while ensuring serious intent.

**Q: How do we compete with lower-priced competitors?**  
A: Sell on value, not price. Our ROI justifies premium positioning. Target customers who value quality and compliance.

**Q: What if customer wants custom pricing outside these tiers?**  
A: Enterprise tier is fully customizable. For Pro/Starter, only approved discounts allowed.

**Q: How do we handle price increases for existing customers?**  
A: Annual increases capped at 5% or CPI, whichever is lower. Communicated 90 days in advance.
